Answers to the Most Common Questions We Receive - Everything You Need to Know!

    How can I pause my tiffin?

    You can pause your tiffin deliveries by informing us on WhatsApp before 7:00 PM, one day prior to your scheduled delivery.

  • location_on

    How do I change my delivery address?

    To change your delivery address, please inform us on WhatsApp before 7:00 PM, one day prior, and we will update it accordingly.

  • sms

    How will I receive my tracking link?

    Your tracking link will be shared with you via SMS on the day of delivery so you can track your order in real time.

  • calendar_month

    Can I choose specific delivery days?

    Yes, you can choose your preferred delivery days. Please inform us on WhatsApp before 7:00 PM, one day prior.

  • edit_note

    Can I customize my tiffin plan?

    Yes, you can customize your tiffin plan based on your preferences. Contact us on WhatsApp and our team will assist you.

  • verified

    Is the chicken halal?

    Yes, we use halal-certified chicken in all our non-vegetarian meals.

  • local_shipping

    Are there any delivery charges?

    We provide free delivery. However, upstairs delivery is charged at $1 per delivery.

  • restaurant

    Can I select veg on non-veg plans?

    Yes, you can choose vegetarian meals for specific days even if you are on a non-veg plan through our item selection form.

  • local_fire_department

    What is the spice level of food?

    Our food is generally low to moderately spicy. If you prefer spicier food, you can opt for our spicy chutney add-on.

  • menu_book

    Does the menu remain the same?

    No, our menu changes every week to ensure variety, freshness, and a better dining experience.
